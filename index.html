Frequently Asked Questions About Team Building Packages

What is your maximum capacity and how many pools do you have?
Aces Private Resort can accommodate up to 100 guests comfortably. We have 2 main pools - a 4ft deep main pool perfect for adults and team activities, and a 3ft kiddie pool. For team building events, we recommend groups of 20-80 people to ensure everyone can participate effectively in activities.
How much are your team building packages and what's included?
Our team building packages start at ₱1,500 per person (minimum of 20 pax) which includes: 8-hour exclusive use of the resort, 2 professional facilitators, complete team building equipment, 5 team activities of your choice, certificates, and basic sound system. We also offer premium packages at ₱2,000 per person that include lunch buffet and afternoon snacks. Custom packages can be arranged based on your specific needs and group size.
How far in advance should we book for a corporate team building event?
We recommend booking at least 3-4 weeks in advance for corporate team building events, especially during peak seasons (March-May and October-December). A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking date. For groups of 50 or more, we suggest booking 6-8 weeks ahead to ensure availability and proper preparation of activities.
Do you provide catering services or can we bring our own food?
We offer in-house catering services with various Filipino and international menu options starting at ₱450 per person. You also have the option to bring your own food with a minimal corkage fee of ₱1,000. If you choose our catering, we need final headcount and menu selection at least 1 week before your event. We have a full kitchen and serving staff to handle your group's needs.
What happens if it rains during our scheduled team building day?
We have contingency plans for rainy weather. Our covered pavilion can accommodate up to 100 people for indoor team building activities. If severe weather is forecast, we can reschedule your event up to 48 hours before with no penalty. For light rain, we have modified versions of our activities that can be conducted under shelter. We also provide free rescheduling if there's a typhoon signal in Angeles City on your event date.
