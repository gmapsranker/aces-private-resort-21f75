Frequently Asked Questions About Holiday Party Packages

What is your maximum capacity for private events and do you require a minimum number of guests?
Our resort can comfortably accommodate up to 100 guests for private events. For holiday party packages, we require a minimum of 20 guests to book the venue exclusively. The main pool area can fit 50-60 people, while our covered pavilion area accommodates up to 100 guests. For intimate gatherings, our smaller function rooms can host 20-30 people comfortably.
How far in advance should we book for Christmas and New Year events?
For holiday season bookings (December 15 - January 2), we recommend reserving at least 3-4 months in advance as these dates fill up quickly in Pampanga. A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your preferred date. Last-minute bookings during peak season are rarely available, but you can contact us at 0917-XXX-XXXX to check current availability.
What's included in your basic holiday party package and how much does it cost?
Our basic holiday package starts at ₱25,000 for 30 guests (₱833 per head) and includes: 6-hour exclusive use of the resort, basic sound system, use of swimming pools and karaoke facilities, 2 lifeguards on duty, clean restrooms with shower areas, and 30 food coupons for our standard buffet menu. Additional guests are charged ₱800 per person. We also offer premium packages with upgraded menu options and additional amenities.
Is overnight stay allowed and what are the check-in/check-out times?
Yes, we offer overnight accommodations in our air-conditioned rooms. Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM the next day. For day use, operating hours are from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged for an additional fee, subject to availability. We have 5 rooms that can accommodate 4-6 persons each.
Can we bring our own food and drinks? Is there a corkage fee?
Yes, you can bring your own food and drinks with applicable corkage fees: ₱1,500 for outside catering, ₱500 for cake, and ₱50 per bottle for alcoholic beverages. However, we recommend our in-house catering service which offers special holiday menus featuring Kapampangan specialties. We also have a full-service bar and restaurant on-site. No cooking is allowed within the resort premises for safety reasons.
