Frequently Asked Questions About Birthday and Debut Packages

How much does it cost to rent the resort for a birthday party package?
Our basic birthday package at Aces Private Resort starts at ₱15,000 for 30 guests, which includes 8-hour exclusive use of the resort, use of swimming pools, function hall, and basic sound system. Additional guests are ₱300/person. We also offer premium packages starting at ₱25,000 that include catering, decorated party area, and host/emcee. Please note that peak season (March-May) rates may be higher. A 50% deposit is required to secure your booking.
How far in advance should I book for a debut celebration?
For debut celebrations, we recommend booking at least 3-4 months in advance, especially if you're planning for a weekend date or during peak months (December-February and March-May). This ensures you get your preferred date and gives enough time to plan the details. For debuts, we require a minimum 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your date, with full payment due 2 weeks before the event.
Is there parking available at the resort and how many cars can it accommodate?
Yes, we have secure parking within the resort premises that can accommodate up to 20 vehicles. Additional parking is available along our private road that can fit 10-15 more cars. We provide a security guard during events to monitor the parking area. For large events, we can arrange overflow parking with our partner establishment nearby.
What happens if it rains during our outdoor event?
We have contingency plans for rainy weather. Our covered function hall can accommodate up to 100 guests, and we have several large pavilions that can be quickly set up with side curtains. We also provide free rescheduling if there's a typhoon signal announced by PAGASA specifically for Pampanga area 48 hours before your event. This should be discussed with our events coordinator at least 2 days before your scheduled date.
Can we bring our own food and drinks, or do we need to use your catering service?
You have the option to bring your own food and drinks with a minimal corkage fee of ₱1,500. However, if you choose our in-house catering service, we waive the corkage fee and offer special package rates starting at ₱450 per person for a full buffet menu. Please note that if you bring alcoholic beverages, there's an additional corkage fee of ₱500 per kind. For food safety reasons, we require all external caterers to present proper business permits and food handling certificates.
