Frequently Asked Questions About Catering Services

What are your current day tour and overnight rates for the private resort?
Our day tour rates (8am-5pm) start at ₱8,000 for up to 15 persons, while overnight packages (2pm-12nn next day) start at ₱12,000 for the same group size. Both packages include exclusive use of the pool, function areas, and basic amenities. Additional guests are charged ₱300 per person. During peak seasons (December-May) and holidays, rates may increase by 20%. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date.
Do you provide catering services, and can we bring our own food?
Yes, we offer in-house catering services starting at ₱450 per person with a minimum of 20 pax. Our Pampanga-style menu includes classic Filipino dishes and local specialties. If you prefer to bring your own food, we charge a corkage fee of ₱1,500 for external catering. Use of our kitchen facilities and basic utensils is included in the resort rental. Please note that cooking areas must be cleaned after use.
How many people can the resort accommodate and what facilities are available?
Aces Private Resort can comfortably accommodate up to 50 people. We have a main pool (4ft-6ft deep), kiddie pool, 2 air-conditioned rooms that can sleep 4-6 people each, covered pavilion for events, full kitchen, 3 bathrooms with hot/cold showers, karaoke system, and parking for 8 vehicles. The entire property is secured with CCTV and 24-hour security personnel.
What is your booking and payment policy for reservations?
To secure your booking, we require a 50% non-refundable deposit payable via bank transfer or GCash. The remaining balance should be paid upon check-in. For cancellations made 7 days before the event, the deposit can be moved to a future booking within 3 months. We also collect a ₱2,000 security deposit (refundable) upon check-in for any potential damages.
How far are you from Angeles City proper and what's the best way to get there?
We're located in Balibago, Angeles City, approximately 10 minutes from SM Clark and 15 minutes from Clark International Airport. From Manila, take NLEX and exit at Clark (around 1.5 hours drive). We're easily accessible via Grab or local tricycles from Angeles city proper. We provide detailed directions and a Google Maps pin upon booking confirmation. Our location is secure and well-lit, with several convenience stores within walking distance.
