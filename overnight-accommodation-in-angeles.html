Frequently Asked Questions About Overnight Accommodation

What is your maximum capacity and how many rooms does the resort have?
Aces Private Resort can accommodate up to 25-30 guests comfortably. We have 4 air-conditioned rooms: two family rooms that can fit 6-8 people each, and two standard rooms for 4-5 people each. The resort also includes a spacious living area and outdoor spaces for gatherings.
How much is the overnight rate and what's included?
Our overnight rates start at ₱8,000 (22-hour use) for up to 15 persons during weekdays, and ₱10,000 on weekends. This includes full access to the swimming pool, karaoke system, kitchen facilities, and all rooms. Additional guests beyond 15 persons are charged ₱300 per head. Check-in time is 2 PM, and check-out is at 12 noon the next day.
Do you allow cooking and bringing of food and drinks?
Yes, we allow guests to bring and prepare their own food and beverages. Our resort has a fully equipped kitchen with a refrigerator, rice cooker, and gas stove. We also provide basic cooking utensils and dinnerware. For alcoholic beverages, we just require responsible drinking and compliance with our noise policies after 10 PM out of respect for our neighbors.
How far are you from Clark International Airport and Angeles city center?
We're conveniently located just 15 minutes (about 6 kilometers) from Clark International Airport and 10 minutes from Angeles city center. Our exact location is in Diamond Subdivision, a quiet residential area that's still accessible to major establishments. We can assist with arranging transportation from the airport or providing directions.
What is your reservation and cancellation policy?
To confirm a booking, we require a 50% down payment via bank transfer or GCash. The remaining balance is due upon check-in. For cancellations made at least 7 days before the check-in date, we offer a full refund of the down payment. Cancellations made 3-6 days prior receive a 50% refund, while cancellations within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable. We strongly recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend stays.
